Shooting Portals

Embark on a multi-galaxy journey, and survive using portal technology.

Unity, Microsoft Azure Playfab

Shooting Portals is a PC 2D puzzle-based platformer designed for youths in the age range of 13-25. The game revolves around Pulse, an inventor residing in the Portal Galaxy. His dog, Orbit, accidentally entered into an unknown galaxy. Hence, it is up to you to rescue Orbit. Utilise Pulse's portal-based mechanics to your advantage, and survive through 10 levels filled with menacing beasts.

neatnotes

Seamless note-taking, right at your fingertips.

Flutter, Firebase, Figma

neatnotes is a notes-taking Android mobile application that is designed for users to easily create and manage their notes on the go. It has a simplistic yet functional interface, providing features such as user authentication (email and Google sign-in), notes-taking and PDF generation.

ShareCar

Sharing cars, has never been easier.

Contextual Inquiries, User Journey Maps, Wireframing, Figma, Heuristic Evaluation

ShareCar is a car sharing mobile application UX design project. It connects car owners with potential customers while protecting the interests of both parties. For example, a rating system is implemented to help customers avoid errant owners, and a reward mechanism incentivises customers to take good care of the cars during the transaction.
